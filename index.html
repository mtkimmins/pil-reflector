PIL Reflection Compiler

The PIL is to be submitted on CANVAS as a single text block without titles. It is potentially annoying to write each section separately and copy-paste it all together. This compiler will provide a space to write your PIL and later compile it into a single chunk of text to copy into CANVAS for submission. Text is saved locally to each device, so you can write when convenient and not worry about losing progress. The form can be cleared for subsequent PIL reflections.



What happened that was surprising, disappointing, exciting, challenging, or impactful?


What did you say or do that resonated with the patient or yourself? What do you think supported you in your interaction?


What do you want to try differently next time? What additional learning do you need to do to improve or gain confidence?


Feedback from your peer (if present)?


Feedback from the Lab Facilitator (if present)?


Brief notes: Write some initial thoughts, behaviours, perspectives, or goals that you would want to explore on a deeper level.



Your compiled note will appear below. It will also be copied to your clipboard upon compiling.
